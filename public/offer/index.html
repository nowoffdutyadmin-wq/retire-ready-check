Now Off Duty

The Calm Retirement Program

Four weeks to build the practice that makes the rest of this chapter feel the way you planned.

A small-group program for people who are done waiting for the calm to arrive on its own.

Over four weeks, Chris works with a small group of people who've recognised the same thing: that the retirement they planned doesn't feel the way they expected, and that the standard advice has fallen short of the actual transition. The program teaches two specific practices: one that helps you come off duty, one that quiets the mind. It also gives you a structure and an accountability partner to make sure they stick. Most people notice a difference within the first week. By week four, the practice is simply part of their day.
